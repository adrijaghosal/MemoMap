❓ Help & Support

Find answers to your questions

🌍
🚀 How do I create my first memory?

Creating your first memory is easy! Click on the "Add Memory" button in the sidebar. Fill in the title, location, date, choose your mood, add photos, and write your story. Click "Save Memory" and your memory will appear on your dashboard and map!

Tip: Add photos to make your memories more vivid!
🗺️ How does the Memory Map work?

The Memory Map automatically plots all your memories on an interactive world map based on their locations. You can click on any pin to view memory details, zoom in/out, and filter memories by mood. Each mood has a different colored pin!

📸 Can I add multiple photos to a memory?

Yes! You can add up to 10 photos per memory. Simply click the upload area and select multiple images, or drag and drop them directly. All photos will be saved with your memory and displayed in a beautiful gallery.

🎙️ How do I add a voice note to my memory?

When adding or editing a memory, click the "Record Voice" button. Allow microphone access when prompted, then click again to stop recording. Your voice note will be saved and can be played back anytime!

Tip: Record your thoughts and feelings in the moment for a more emotional experience years later!
🔒 What is a Time Capsule?

A Time Capsule is a special memory that you can lock for 1, 5, or 10 years (or custom years). Until the unlock date, the memory is hidden and can only be viewed when the time comes. It's perfect for writing letters to your future self!

👤 How do I change my profile picture?

Go to your Profile page and click on the camera icon on your avatar. You can either choose from our emoji collection or upload your own photo. Click "Save" to update your profile picture!

🔐 How do I change my password?

Go to Settings → Account → Change Password. Enter your current password and your new password, then confirm. Click "Update Password" to save changes.

🗑️ How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. You'll need to type "DELETE" to confirm. Warning: This action is permanent and cannot be undone!

🔧 My photos aren't uploading. What should I do?

Make sure your images are under 5MB and in JPEG or PNG format. If you're still having issues, try refreshing the page or using a different browser. Chrome and Firefox work best with MemoMap.

🔧 The map isn't loading properly.

Try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache. If the issue persists, check your internet connection. The map requires an active internet connection to load tiles.

🔧 I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

On the login page, click "Forgot Password?". Enter your email address and we'll send you a reset link. Follow the instructions to create a new password.
